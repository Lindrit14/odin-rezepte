Ice Cream

Go on, indulge yourself with this exotic combination of rose and saffron. Serve this ice cream for dessert to your guests next time you host a party and wow them! Cashews and almonds also go well with this ice cream.

Ingrediants

  • 1 pinch saffron
  • 2 tablespoons warm milk
  • 1 ½ quarts vanilla ice cream, softened
  • 1 ½ tablespoons rose syrup
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • ¼ cup chopped pistachio nuts

Steps

    Step 1
  1. Add the saffron to the warm milk and set aside for 15 minutes.
    2. Step 2
  2. Combine the softened ice cream, saffron with milk, rose syrup, cardamom, and pistachios in a large bowl.
    3. Step 3
  3. Stir until the ice cream is smooth and pink in color. Scoop the ice cream back into the container and freeze 2 hours, or until ready to eat.
